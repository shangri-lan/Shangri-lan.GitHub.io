Ruoaln Yang
Entrepreneur / Media Artist / Feminist
I’m the founder of MarsBox, the first e-commerce and sex-education platform for sexual minorities in China.


Before beginning the journey to run the business, I got my bachelor of arts degree from University of Nottingham, Ningbo, China in English Studies with Applied Linguistic.


I’m also interested in media art, especially in interactive installations and Immersive Experience. I figure out ways to give form to the experience and understanding of the world as I see it. It’s my mission to communicate the minorities, sexual and racial, with the rest of the world with my artistic insights.
